### DATA ANALYTICS AND STATISTICS PROJECT

I have hosted some of my finished and unfinished works in my [github profile](https://github.com/varadan13/Projects_and_Notes)

-   [Kerala Assembly Election Analytics](https://github.com/varadan13/Projects_and_Notes/tree/main/Kerala-Assembly-Election-Analytics)
    > -   The 2021 Kerala Legislative Assembly election is scheduled to be held in May of this year to elect 140 MLAs to the 15th Kerala Legislative Assembly.    
    > -   I am not a political pundit but I thought to myself it would be nice to know how various individuals and various other political parties fared in the previous elections.   
    > -   The data was collected from the website of Election Commission of India. The ipython notebook is semi interactive.  
    > -   In the future I plan to collect demographic data from Registar General office of India and conduct a more detailed study combining both the data set.  

-   [Flight delay prediction](https://github.com/varadan13/Projects_and_Notes/blob/main/flight_delay_predictions.ipynb)  
    > -   I developed a model aimed at predicting flight delays. The intended purpose of this project was not to obtain the best possible prediction but rather to emphasize on the various steps needed to build such a model.  
    > -   During the EDA, I intended to create good quality figures from which the information would be easily accessible at a first glance.  

-   [A Climate Change Visualisation Project](https://github.com/varadan13/Projects_and_Notes/tree/main/A-Climate-Change-Visualisation-Project)    
    > -   This project is unfinished and the questions I am trying to tackle here are:
    >     > -   What are the ten most countries that suffer from temperature change mostly in the last ten years?  
    >     > -   What are the ten countries that suffer from temperature change at the very least in the last ten years?
    >     > -   Is there any significant difference between seasons? 
    >     > -   What is the trend of temperature change in the world?
    > -   The main libraries I am using are Pandas, NumPy for data wrangling and Matplotlib, Plotly for data visualization.   

-   Meta-analysis to evaluate the success rates of various surgical techniques for socket preservation in the esthetic zone.  
    > -   I had to unwillingly help a friend of friend of mine during the lockdown period to run the inference part of this project and I don't think I did a good job but I value the effort that I took to first learn what is meta analysis and how to solve various practical difficulties involved in this project.  
    > -   I was able to successfully extract all the possible treatment effects from the sampled research materials and then use random effect model to draw inference.  
    > -   The software that I used in this project was Revman.
