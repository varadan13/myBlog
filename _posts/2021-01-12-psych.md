### Questions

> Are secrets experienced as physical burdens? How does secrets change our perception or expectations from the world?  

### Introduction

> Everyone has secrets don't we? Some of us may conceal dark secrets within us and How does these secrets affect us? The report titled "The Physical Burdens of Secrecy" by Slepian et al. investigates how secrets effect us by running four different experiments and according to them "Bearing secrets influenced visual perception and action, made physical tasks seem more effortful, and decreased willingness to provide physical help."  

### The Hypothesis

> Secrets are metaphorically considered as burdens. When people hold secrets they act as if they are physically burdened.
